About Me

My name is Erich Stremel. I was born in Burgenlenfeld, Germany in 1989. I moved to Kansas when I was 2 years old, and was raised there until I was 13. When I turned 13, I then moved down to New Braunfels, TX and was raised the rest of the time there.

I have lived here in Denver now for going on 5 years, and I love it. I worked in Oil and Gas for 7 years, and decided it was not the right industry for me. I then decided to change my career path to coding and web development.

Some of my favorite activities include: snowboarding, camping, hunting, and video games. I have a 4 year old dog named Jaeger who brings me a lot of joy in my life.
